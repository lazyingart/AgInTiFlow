## AgInTiFlow

Project-aware, low-cost agents for art, writing, hardware, software, science, and industry.

AgInTiFlow is a low-cost, project-aware agent workspace for hybrid wet-dry R&D, hardware-aware intelligence, and industrial automation. From wet-lab planning to dry-lab analysis, from hardware control to software pipelines, and from microscopy, drones, and robots to production scripts, it helps agents work through API, Web, or CLI with SCS supervision, AAPS workflows, guarded execution, and durable evidence.

## AAPS

Project-oriented programming for art, writing, hardware, software, science, and industry.

AAPS is a prompt-native programing language and visual studio for turning prompts into structured, verifiable pipelines that connect wet and dry experiments, hardware and software, and human intent with executable agent work. It organizes agents, tasks, typed inputs, declared outputs, executable actions, validation gates, recovery steps, and durable artifacts.
